Simple rules to crack GD

  • Train yourself to be a good listener.
  • Acknowledge that everyone has something valuable to say.
  • When talking in a GD, yourjob is to articulate your point of view in such a way that is easy for others to understand.
  • Inculcate a thoughts and good habit of structuring your thoughts and presenting them logically.
  • The only way to prepare is to read more, develop a keen interest in current affairs.
  • Writing essays on variety of topics is a good practice in improving your language.
  • Seek opportunities to discuss these in groups.
  • Learn to be open-minded and recognize the fact that people think differently about issues.
  • Train your mind to think analytically.
  • Keep eye contact while speaking.
  • Initiate the GD.
  • Allow others to speak.
  • Speak clearly.
  • Listen carefully to others.
  • Do not go into the depth of the topic.
  • Speak sensibly.
  • Maintain Positive attitude.
  • Make sure you bring the discussion on track.
  • One has to dress formally.
