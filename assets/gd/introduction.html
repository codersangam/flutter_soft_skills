Introduction

Nowadays Group Discussion is being extensively used along with personal interviews for the final selection of the candidates. It plays a vital role in selecting the best among the best.

Group Discussion helps in choosing the socially suitable candidates among the academically superior achievers. It is one of the best tools to study the behavioral and attitudinal responses of the participants.

  • Group discussion in simple words means discussion among a group of people.
  • The group will have 5-12 members who will express their views freely, frankly in a friendly manner on a topic.
  • Within a time limit of 20 to 30 minutes, the abilities of the members are measured.
