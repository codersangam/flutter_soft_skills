Introduction

Nowadays Group Discussion is extensively used along with personal interviews the final selection of the candidates. It plays a main role in selecting the best among the best.

Group Discussion plays an important part. It helps in choosing the socially suitable candidates among the academically superior achievers. It is one of the best tools to study the behavioral and attitudinal responses of the participants.

In short, the GD panel is testing whether you know the topic well, are able to present your point of view in a logical manner, are interested in understanding what others feel about the same subject and are able to conduct yourself with grace in a group situation.

Group discussion in simple words means discussion among a group of people. The group will have 5-12 members who II express their views freely, frankly and in a lily manner on a topic.
