After Interview

  • Immediately say thank you to interviewer and offer a firm handshake and say nice meeting you.
  • Be prepared to attend two or more interview rounds at the same company. If you are called back for another interview, it means that they are interested in you so keep up the good work.
  • Evaluate the interview
  • What questions were asked?
  • What did you say that seemed to interest the interviewer?
  • Did you present your Knowledge, Skills and Abilities well?
  • Did you learn what you need to know about this job?
  • What did you forget to say?
  • Did you get a compliment from the interviewer?
  • Do you know what the next step is in the hiring process?
